Welcome to MyCar, build by Adrian. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

  • Location Data: We access your device's precise (GPS) and approximate location to provide safety insights, track driving metrics, and offer location-based features.
  • Camera Access: We use your device's camera for object detection and real-time safety monitoring (Risk Engine). Image processing is performed on-device via ML Kit.
  • Device Information: We may collect technical data such as device model, OS version, and app usage statistics to improve performance.

2. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data used to identify you:

  • ML Kit: For on-device intelligence and object detection.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To analyze driving behavior and provide "Safety Insights."
  • To detect potential risks on the road using the camera.
  • To show relevant advertisements.
  • To maintain and improve app functionality.

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Permissions

You can revoke Camera and Location permissions at any time through your device settings, though some features may stop functioning.
